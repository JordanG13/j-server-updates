## Installation Notes:
1. Open launcher
2. Click Minecraft folder on the right
3. Delete the `config`, `mods`, and `resourcepack` folders
4. Copy/paste files from 2.0.0.zip into the current folder (.minecraft)
5. After loading Minecraft, go to Options->Resource Packs and enable `Towers_Of_The_Wild_Reworked`
6. All done!
