## Installation Notes:
1. Open launcher
1. Click Minecraft folder on the right
1. Delete the `config`, `mods`, and `resourcepack` folders
1. Copy/paste files from 2.0.0.zip into the current folder (.minecraft)
1. After loading Minecraft, go to Options->Resource Packs and enable `Towers_Of_The_Wild_Reworked`
1. All done!
